Over the past 4 years at CSTU, we have walked this journey together—a journey of dreams and learning. We faced countless challenges, developed ourselves in many aspects, and built friendships that will last a lifetime.

Year 1: The Beginning

In the first year, everything felt new and exciting. We adapted to university life, learned the ropes of studying, and formed bonds with friends who became our lifelong companions.

Year 2: Growth

The second year was a time of learning and developing new skills. Whether it was teamwork, participating in workshops, or joining activities, we gained valuable experiences that shaped who we are.

Year 3: Challenges

The third year brought new challenges. We worked on major projects that required knowledge, creativity, and close collaboration, pushing us to grow even further.

Year 4: Success

The final year was filled with pride and accomplishment. We completed our studies and celebrated our achievements while cherishing the memories we’ve created together.
